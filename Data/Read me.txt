About datasets:
   The code for simulating a three-dimensional coupled system and an eight-dimensional logistic system are provided in 'utils.py'. 
   The datasets of DREAM4 in silico Network Challenge, food chain network, Hong Kong air pollution and cardiovascular disease dataset are provided in folder 'data'. 
   The complete time series data of real neural networks and rat circadian rhythm gene expression data can be found in the corresponding references or obtained from the corresponding author on reasonable request.
